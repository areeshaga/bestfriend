dear best friend,

i don't say this enough but — thank you. thank you for picking up when i call at weird hours. for remembering the small things. for laughing at my terrible jokes even when they're genuinely awful.

you have this way of making ordinary moments feel like they matter. like we're in a movie that nobody else is watching, and honestly that's my favorite kind.

i hope you know that you are so very loved. not in a loud, dramatic way (though sometimes that too) but in the steady, reliable, always-there kind of way.

with all the love,
me

reminder: you are literally the funniest person i know and i will not be taking questions

p.s. thank you for always knowing when i need silence and when i need you to talk forever
